\begin{abstract}
We present nano-coder, an open-source benchmark comparing parameter-efficient fine-tuning
methods---from LoRA and DoRA through quantum-\emph{inspired} tensor networks (TT-LoRA, TRG, MPO)---
for sub-1B code models on JavaScript, HTML, and FreeMarker.
All student models train on teacher-distilled synthetic data with hypothesis-first discipline,
bond dimension $\chi$ as the central compression axis, and honest evidence labeling
(Established vs.\ QuantumInspired vs.\ Speculative).
\end{abstract}
